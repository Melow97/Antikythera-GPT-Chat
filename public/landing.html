What's inside

💬

Multi-model chat

Talk to OpenAI, DeepSeek, or run models fully locally/offline with Ollama — switch any time from a quick picker at the top of the app.

🔌

App connections

Real OAuth connections to Google, Microsoft, GitHub, Slack, and Zoom, so the agent can work with the tools you already use.

🔎

Deep web search

Toggle on live web search before a message sends, and get answers grounded in current sources with citations.

🧩

Customizable workspace

Add your own sidebar sections — notes, a calendar, or freeform space — and shape the app around how you actually work.

Built to flex to your work

Content moderation support

Stay on top of incoming work

Once signed in, you can opt in to notifications about incoming tasks tied to your work email — or just personal reminders, your call. Turn it on any time from Settings after you sign in.

This is an opt-in preference you control in-app — it's not automatically enabled, and it's a notification preference today rather than a fully automated inbox agent.

⚙️

Built in the open

Antikythera GPT Chat is open source, published on GitHub for anyone to read, run, fork, or improve. We think the tools people rely on for AI should be inspectable rather than opaque — code you can actually look at, not just a promise from a vendor.

If that's a future worth building, come take a look at the repository: read the code, file an issue, suggest a feature, or open a pull request. Whether you're kicking the tires as a user or digging into the source as a contributor, it's all welcome.
