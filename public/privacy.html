Antikythera GPT Chat

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Antikythera GPT Chat is a small, early-stage project. This page explains, in plain language, what information the app actually collects, how it's used, and what your options are. It describes the app's real, current behavior — not a generic template.

What we collect

  • Account info: your email, first and last name, and a password — stored as a one-way bcrypt hash, never in plain text.
  • Google sign-in: if you use "Continue with Google," we receive your Google account's email, name, and profile picture URL.
  • Session cookie: a secure, HTTP-only cookie that keeps you signed in. It contains no personal data itself, just a reference to your session.
  • Connected apps (optional): if you connect Google, Microsoft, GitHub, Slack, or Zoom from the Connections page, we store the resulting access/refresh tokens, encrypted at rest, only to perform the actions those integrations are for. Nothing is connected unless you explicitly click "Connect."
  • Chat conversations: stored only in your own browser (localStorage) — never on our servers. Clearing your browser data deletes them permanently; we don't keep a copy and can't recover them for you.
  • Message content, in transit: what you type is sent to the AI model (Ollama, running locally or via Ollama Cloud depending on how this instance is configured) to generate a reply. If you turn on Deep search, Flights, or Hotels for a message, the relevant part of that message is also sent to the corresponding third-party API (Ollama's web search, Kiwi.com, or Amadeus) to fetch live results.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data to anyone.
  • We don't use your chats to train any model — we don't have a copy of them to begin with.
  • We don't share connected-app tokens with anyone besides the provider they're for.

Third-party services involved

Depending on which features you use, your data may pass through: Ollama (chat and web search), Resend (account emails), Kiwi.com (flight search), Amadeus (hotel search), and whichever providers you personally connect (Google, Microsoft, GitHub, Slack, Zoom). Each has its own privacy practices we don't control — we only send what's needed for the feature you're using, at the moment you use it.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your account data at any time — this is a small project without a self-service "delete my account" button yet, so email us and we'll handle it directly, typically within a few days.

Children's privacy

This app isn't directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we'll remove it.

Security

Passwords are hashed, sessions are HTTP-only, and connected-app tokens are encrypted at rest. No online service can guarantee perfect security, but we take reasonable, real steps rather than just claiming to.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continuing to use the app after a change means you accept the update.

Questions, feedback, or a feature idea?

This is genuinely a two-way conversation, not just a legal formality — if you've got a privacy question, a feature request, a bug report, or just want to tell us what's working or not, we'd like to hear it: antikytheragptchat@gmail.com.
